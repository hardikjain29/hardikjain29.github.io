More about the project

Treebo is a website where you can look up an hotel in the city you want and go ahead and make a booking.

I was in the making of their Desktop and Msite(PWA) which gets around 14k visitors per day

The Treebo PWA was presented in Google IO in 2017 for it's great performance on low and end and slow connectivity devices.

I along with a team of 5 front-end developers build stuff to all their platforms.(Desktop, PWA, (Android & Ios) React Native)

The front-end stack has React.js,GraphQl,Webpack, Docker.
