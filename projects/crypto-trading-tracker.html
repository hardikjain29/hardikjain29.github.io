More about the project

Cryto Trading Tracker was a side project of mine.

It started when the bubble of crypto was getting bigger in late 2017 and early 2018. The trade numbers had sky rocketed and every one used to open up their respective trade platform and check how much richer they are getting and how close are they to become a millionaire. One of the markets platform was Koinex. People at workplaces kept going to visit this site and check their numbers.

Something hit my mmind. What if there was a tracker right up your taskbar so never had to open the browser explicitly and visit the site.

So I made a desktop app which would show the current trade amount of all the coins which would be updated every 10 seconds.

Later, Something else hit my mind. The crypto market is very volatile. Anything could happen at any moment and you could easily miss it. So I made a notification system which would give you an alert on the desktop that your desired coin trade value has reached and you could swiftly go and Buy/Sell the coin and hence not miss any opportunites.

It was built with ReactJs, Electron, Node.js
