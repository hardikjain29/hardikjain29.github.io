More about the project

Fanbytes is a bridge which connects Brands to Influencers.
The platform contains two parts, one for Brands and other for Influencers.

I was in the making of both platforms wherein the brands put in the their request for an ideal advert and the Influencers create their videos and upload them to qualify.

I along with the CTO were the only frontend devs for the platform. Worked to create the dashboards and build the redesign of their Landing Page.

The front-end stack has React.js and Redux as the state management, SCSS for CSS and axios to call the API's.

The project went on for 5 months and has set to sail for high growth and reach.
They are scaling rapidly and have been backed by Founders Factory for their road to success.
